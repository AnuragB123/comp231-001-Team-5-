WELCOME "UserName" TO THE ALL IN ONE STUDY APPLICATION!

Our Application is an All in One Study Application that has everything a student needs to time manage and work smartly

We have 3 Main Features: Checklist, Calendar and Grader

Checklist: Students and Instructors will be allowed to add different tasks to checklists and would be able to arrange them to three versions, To Do, Ongoing and Done

Calendar: Students and Instructors will be able to add "events" to the calendar to schedule events like classes, labs due dates, assignment due dates, test/quiz dates and more

Grader: Student and Instructors will be able to add their grades for their subjects and calculate what their average grade would be
